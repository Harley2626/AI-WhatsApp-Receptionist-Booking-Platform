# Wazzy — AI WhatsApp Receptionist & Booking Platform

## Vision

Build the simplest possible AI receptionist for South African service
businesses.

The platform should allow customers to interact entirely through
WhatsApp while giving business owners a lightweight dashboard for setup,
reporting and management.

The product is **not** intended to be a full ERP or CRM. It should solve
one problem exceptionally well:

> Help businesses never miss another customer enquiry or booking.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Core Principles

## 1. Do Not Over Engineer

Every feature must answer:

-   Does this directly help the customer book?
-   Does this save the business owner time?
-   Will at least 80% of customers use it?

If the answer is no, postpone it.

### Rules

-   Build the MVP first.
-   Avoid unnecessary configuration.
-   Avoid complex workflows.
-   Avoid enterprise features.
-   Prefer automation over user input.
-   Prefer sensible defaults.
-   One click is better than three.
-   One screen is better than five.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Design Philosophy

The platform should feel:

-   Friendly
-   Fast
-   Invisible
-   Mobile-first
-   WhatsApp-first

The customer should feel like they are chatting with a helpful
receptionist, not using software.

The business owner should feel like the software "just works."

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Customer Journey

1.  Message the business on WhatsApp.
2.  AI greets the customer.
3.  AI answers FAQs.
4.  AI checks availability.
5.  Customer books.
6.  AI collects payment (optional, full amount or a deposit — set per service by the business).
7.  Confirmation sent.
8.  Reminder sent.
9.  Follow-up after appointment.

Core flow in one line: **Chat → Book → Pay → Confirmation → Reminders.**

No app download required.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Business Owner Journey

1.  Sign up.
2.  Enter business details.
3.  Add services.
4.  Set business hours.
5.  Connect calendar.
6.  Connect WhatsApp.
7.  Connect payments.
8.  Go live.

Target setup time: under 10 minutes.

# Product Test

Before adding any feature, ask:

1.  Does it help customers book?
2.  Does it save business owners time?
3.  Can it be explained in one sentence?
4.  Will most businesses use it?
5.  Can it wait until Phase 2?

If the answer to (5) is yes, do not build it in the MVP.
